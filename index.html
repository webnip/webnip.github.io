FOR SALE

A rare opportunity to own a 3 bedroom + maids room/ 3 bathroom condo (122.47 sqm) plus a tandem parking slot (2 car slots), at one of most beautiful and convenient locations in Bonifacio Global City, Trion Towers. The unit is 5 years old, recently lovingly renovated to a very high level of quality fixtures and fitting. The unit is sold as semi furnished with high quality, all new branded appliances, Panasonic fridge, Bosch dishwasher, Samsung washing machine, Panasonic microwave. The unit comes equipped with Carrier eco-friendly 'window-type' air-con in each room. The property benefits from being an end unit where the balcony wraps around the whole of the unit in an ‘L’ shape formation, which is only one of six units throughout the Trion Towers complex like this. Apart from the master bedroom, bedrooms No. 2 & 3 as well as the dining room have doors to access the balcony area.

LOCATION

The unit is set in a prime location at the back of Trion Tower 2, which provides a great position to get away from the hustle and bustle of the city life. The unit is only one of six units that has a 30sqm balcony, which has triple aspect views of the lap pool/ leisure pool, SM Aura, BGC high street & the lush greenery of the garden area & American Battle Monument.
